About Me

My journey with data began in the field with Ghana Statistical Service, where I learned the importance of data integrity from the source. This unique perspective allows me to understand that behind every dataset are real people and stories.

I've since developed technical expertise in Python, SQL, and Power BI to help businesses unlock the potential in their data. I bridge the gap between raw data and real-world strategy, combining field-based wisdom with analytical skills to deliver meaningful insights that drive impact.

When I'm not analyzing data, I enjoy exploring new visualization techniques, contributing to open-source data projects, and mentoring aspiring data professionals.
